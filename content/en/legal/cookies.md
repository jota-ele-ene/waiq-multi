## Statement on Cookies

This website **does NOT use cookies** of any kind, whether first-party or third-party.

### What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file that is downloaded to the user’s device when accessing certain websites, with the purpose of storing information related to browsing activity.

### Which cookies does this site use?

**None.** This website does not install, download or use:

- Technical or functional cookies
- Analytics or statistical cookies (such as Google Analytics)
- Advertising or remarketing cookies
- Social media cookies
- Any other type of cookie or tracking identifier

### Why do we not use cookies?

Because this website is purely informative and does not require:

- Storing user browsing preferences
- Collecting statistical information about site usage
- Tracking user behaviour
- Displaying personalised advertising

### User Privacy

As no cookies are used, this website **fully respects users’ privacy**. No information is recorded regarding:

- Browsing activity
- Usage patterns
- Location data
- Behaviour on the Internet
