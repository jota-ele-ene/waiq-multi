The European Commission is considering proposing rules that would restrict EU member governments' use of U.S. cloud platforms to process sensitive government data, according to official sources. This initiative is part of the 'Tech Sovereignty Package' expected to be presented on May 27, aiming to reduce dependence on U.S. cloud providers that currently dominate the European market.
