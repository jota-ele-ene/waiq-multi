The European Central Bank (ECB) has released a comprehensive report delving into the potential impact of tokenization on financial market infrastructures. This study analyzes how representing assets as digital tokens on a blockchain could optimize processes, reduce costs, and increase transaction speed. While the ECB acknowledges the benefits in terms of efficiency and resilience, it also underscores the need for a clear regulatory framework and robust international cooperation to manage associated risks, such as cybersecurity and financial stability. The ECB's assessment is a key indicator of growing institutional interest in Web3 and its ability to redefine traditional business models in the European financial sector.
