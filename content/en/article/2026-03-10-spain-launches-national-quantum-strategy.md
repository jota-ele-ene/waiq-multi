The Spanish government has announced significant investment and a long-term strategic plan for quantum computing, aiming to foster a robust ecosystem that attracts talent and drives innovation. The strategy will focus on creating quantum research nodes, promoting public-private collaboration, and training professionals. This move is seen as a crucial step to secure technological sovereignty for Spain and Europe in a highly strategic field, competing with other global powers in the race to dominate the next generation of computing. The initiative also seeks to generate new business models and industrial applications that benefit the national economy.
