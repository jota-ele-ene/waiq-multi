Bitpanda, Austrian crypto broker, launches Vision Chain, a blockchain designed to comply with European regulations like MiCA and MiFID II, enabling European banks and fintechs to issue and settle tokenized assets. This project represents the convergence of Web3 and traditional finance under European regulatory frameworks, demonstrating new business models in tokenization.
