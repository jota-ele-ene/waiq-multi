Minister Óscar López announced at MWC Barcelona Spain's participation in IPCEI-AI (Important Project of Common European Interest in AI), led by Germany. Nine Spanish companies —including Telefónica, Indra, Multiverse Computing and Quside— have been selected as candidates and will seek European partners at workshops in Berlin on March 10-11.
