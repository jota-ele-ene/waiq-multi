MiCA (Art. 22(4)) prohibits granting interest or benefits linked to holding time for asset-referenced tokens, to avoid competition with bank deposits. WAIQ relevance: less yield-driven adoption, more real utility (payments/settlement/tokenization), and reduced systemic risk.
