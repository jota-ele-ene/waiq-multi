In a move that underscores growing concerns about the social impact of AI, the European Parliament has passed a resolution urging the Commission and member states to establish clear and binding guidelines for the ethical use of AI in public services. The resolution highlights the importance of ensuring that AI applications in areas such as health, education, and justice respect democratic principles, transparency, and accountability. A specific call is made to prevent algorithmic biases and ensure that citizens have the right to human oversight and to challenge automated decisions. This initiative reflects Europe's commitment to leading responsible AI governance, protecting citizens, and fostering trust in these transformative technologies.
