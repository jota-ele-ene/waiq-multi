A consortium of universities, hospitals, and technology companies in Spain has announced the launch of an ambitious pilot project to revolutionize health data management. The initiative aims to implement a federated Artificial Intelligence system operating on a decentralized Web3 infrastructure, ensuring patient data privacy and control. The key component of this project is the integration of post-quantum cryptography solutions to protect sensitive information against potential future quantum computer attacks, thus ensuring the system's longevity and security. This use case is a paradigmatic example of technological convergence, where AI enhances the analysis of large volumes of clinical data without compromising privacy (thanks to federation and Web3), while quantum security fortifies the entire ecosystem. The project aims not only to improve diagnostic and therapeutic efficiency but also to establish a new ethical standard for handling health data in the advanced digital era, with a significant socioeconomic impact by boosting medical research and treatment personalization.
