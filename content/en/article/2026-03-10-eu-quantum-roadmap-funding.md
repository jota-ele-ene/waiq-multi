With the aim of not falling behind in the global race for quantum supremacy, the European Commission has announced a package of measures including significant investments in fundamental research, development of quantum infrastructure, and support for startups and SMEs. The strategic roadmap details priorities in areas such as quantum computing, quantum communication, and quantum sensing. The goal is to foster cross-border collaboration and the training of specialized talent to build a robust European quantum ecosystem. These initiatives are crucial for Europe's digital sovereignty, industrial competitiveness, and its ability to address future challenges in fields such as cybersecurity, medicine, and materials science. The investment is not only directed at research but also at preparing industry for the adoption and scaling of quantum technologies.
