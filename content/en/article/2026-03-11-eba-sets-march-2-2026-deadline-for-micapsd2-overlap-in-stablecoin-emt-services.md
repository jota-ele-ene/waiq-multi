The EBA ends a transition period and requires crypto providers handling electronic money tokens (MiCA stablecoins) to meet conditions or stop services when activities qualify as PSD2 payment services. WAIQ: it regulates crypto–payments convergence and reshapes EU business models (licensing, capital, go-to-market).
