The European Commission has taken a crucial step towards the realization of the AI Act by publishing its first operational guidelines. This initial package focuses on clarifying the definition and criteria for high-risk AI systems, as well as documentation and human oversight requirements. The guidelines also address algorithmic transparency and explainability, seeking to build trust and accountability. The goal is to provide businesses and national authorities with a clear roadmap for adaptation and compliance, ensuring that Europe positions itself as a leader in ethical and responsible AI development, which will directly impact innovation and competitiveness on the continent.
