Spain's Ministry of Economic Affairs and Digital Transformation has unveiled an ambitious investment plan to strengthen the National Quantum Strategy. This initiative seeks to consolidate research and development infrastructure in quantum computing, with a special emphasis on creating a network of centers of excellence and attracting and retaining researchers and scientists. The investment aims not only to accelerate technological innovation but also to foster public-private collaboration and generate new business opportunities in strategic sectors. This move is vital for Spain's digital autonomy and for contributing to the European Union's goal of technological sovereignty in a field as critical as quantum.
