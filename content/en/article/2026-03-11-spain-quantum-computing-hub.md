In a strategic move to reinforce its position in the quantum race, the Spanish government has announced a substantial investment to establish a new center of excellence in quantum computing. This hub will not only focus on research and development of quantum hardware and software but will also seek to foster collaboration between academia, industry, and the public sector. The goal is to create a vibrant ecosystem that attracts top-tier researchers, drives the creation of startups, and facilitates knowledge transfer to commercial applications. This initiative is vital for Spain's ambition to contribute to European technological sovereignty and to boost new economic and employment opportunities in a high-tech sector. The training of qualified professionals and raising awareness about the potential of quantum computing will also be fundamental pillars of the project.
