The tokenization of real-world assets (RWA) is one of the most promising applications of blockchain technology, and its potential is amplified by the integration of Artificial Intelligence. By representing properties, art, real estate, or commodities as digital tokens on a blockchain, greater liquidity, fractionalization, and transaction efficiency are achieved. AI plays a crucial role in the valuation, risk management, and personalization of investment portfolios for these tokenized assets. This approach opens markets to a broader investor base, lowers entry barriers, and promotes new business models in finance, commerce, and investment. However, regulatory challenges and the need for robust infrastructure remain key areas to address to maximize its impact and ensure investor protection.
