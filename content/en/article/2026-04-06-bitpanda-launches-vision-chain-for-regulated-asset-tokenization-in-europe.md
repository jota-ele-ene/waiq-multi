Bitpanda, a Vienna-based fintech company, launches Vision Chain, a blockchain network to connect regulated European banks with tokenized assets. This initiative demonstrates the convergence between Web3 and financial regulation, allowing European institutions to operate under frameworks like MiCA and MiFID II while adopting Optimism-based blockchain infrastructure.
