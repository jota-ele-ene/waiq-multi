The combination of AI's analytical and predictive capabilities with the transparency and decentralization of DAOs is transforming how decisions are made and projects are managed within the Web3 ecosystem. These new models aim to overcome the limitations of traditional governance, offering fairer and more efficient mechanisms for resource allocation, conflict resolution, and protocol evolution. However, ethical and security questions arise regarding the role of AI in DAO autonomy and the potential concentration of algorithmic power. This development represents a fertile ground for innovation in business models and organizational structures that could redefine the future of digital collaboration and the decentralized economy.
