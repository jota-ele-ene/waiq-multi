MiCA's full entry into force represents a turning point for the Web3 ecosystem in Europe. This legislation provides legal certainty for crypto-asset issuers and service providers, while imposing strict requirements in areas such as transparency, authorization, governance, and market abuse prevention. MiCA is expected to boost investor confidence and encourage institutional adoption of crypto-assets, but it will also challenge some companies to adapt to a more rigorous regulatory environment. Experts anticipate market consolidation and a strategic reorientation of many sector players to comply with the new standards, which could strengthen Europe's position as a regulated hub for Web3 innovation.
