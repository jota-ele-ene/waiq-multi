The EDPB's new guidelines on personal data processing in blockchain confirm that GDPR fully applies to permissionless blockchains, but recognize technical solutions like metadata erasure and off-chain storage to comply with the 'right to be forgotten'. This European regulatory evolution embraces decentralized technologies with a technically grounded approach.
